I Call Her Bae and She's My Love

I call her Bae, my heart’s embrace,

A gentle light, my quiet place.

In whispers soft, her name I say,

My love, my dream, my every day.


She’s my dawn, my midnight star,

Close in soul, though worlds afar.

Her laugh, a melody I hold dear,

In moments close and ones unclear.


I call her Bae, my heart’s delight,

My compass true, my northern light.

Through days of sun and nights above,

She is my life, my endless love.


In winter’s chill, she warms my soul,

In summer’s heat, she makes me whole.

A gentle touch, a tender glance,

Our story told in a timeless dance.


I call her Bae, in voice and song,

In quiet moments, where we belong.

She is the moon to my endless night,

The love that fills my world with light.


With every beat, my heart does say,

Forever hers, in every way.

My love, my heart, my sweetest dove,

I call her Bae, she’s my true love.
